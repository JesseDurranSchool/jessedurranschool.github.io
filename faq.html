Where do we ship?

  We ship to Canada, most of the US, most of the European Union, and all of Australia.


What do you sell?

  We are a magazine company first. We sell magazines full of tutorials and information on how computers and robots work. In addition, we sell various kits meant for all ages that teach people how circuits work, as well as how computers can be used.


How do I cancel my subscription?

  You can cancel your subscription in many ways. The easiest method is to enter your customer id into our website, and then select unsubscribe. Your request will be processed before the next payment, so you will not be charged again.


How can I get a refund?

  If you are unsatisfied with your purchase, you can submit a request, which will be reviewed, and your refund will be sent as soon as possible. We offer mail refunds if you would prefer to not enter your payment information, but it requires proof of purchase as well as identification.


Can I order replacement parts?

  We will replace any missing or broken pieces found in your kit. In addition, individual parts can be purchased for expansion or replacement.
