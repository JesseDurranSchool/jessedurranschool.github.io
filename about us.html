Breadboard Magazine is a technology magazine started in 2023 by Jesse Durran. It was started in the small town of Amherst, Nova Scotia. We discuss all things hardware, software, and theory relating to robotics and computers, and we sell merchandise, and kits for the purpose of getting people into computers.

  Our goal is to teach everyone how computers work, and to inspire people to create new things with them. It is our belief that anyone can get into electronics, if they're just willing to try.
